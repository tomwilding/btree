About The Blossom Tree

The Blossom Tree is a bespoke florist based in Market Lavington. As well as a range of existing flower bouquet designs, we can create the perfect flower bouquets to suit any occasion. Just get in contact to let us know your situation and we will take care of the rest! The Blossom Tree also offer custom flower designs for occasions such as weddings. Please see our Occasions page for more details. If there is anything else that we can help you with, please don't hesitate to contact us directly on 01380 813311 or see our Contact page to get in touch.
